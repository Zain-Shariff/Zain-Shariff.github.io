About College

I deem it my special privilege to welcome you to our College, which crossed the fifty Seven mark in its glorious history.

Founded by philanthropists and social workers who took it upon as their life's mission to create opportunities of higher education for the local people, the institution has always striven to enrich the life of its larger community. It has built up a reputation as one of the leading colleges of the state, providing quality education to young aspirants in the Arts, Science, Commerce, and Computer Aplication streams. Besides Under-Graduate courses in these streams, the College is also running a KH Srinivasa Institute of Post-Graduate Studies Centre, which offers MA (Economics), M.Com. and M.Sc. (Mathematics and Chemistry) courses. The research Center (Commerce)will be started from the year 2020-21.

Located in serene, beautiful, natural surroundings, the College has the best kind of material and human resources. It has excellent library, laboratory, audio-visual, hostel, and canteen facilities. It is served by highly qualified and dedicated teachers and office staff. All these make for an incomparably student friendly environment. The College has enabled thousands of students to excel not only in academics but in cultural and sports activities too. Our alumni have excelled not only as professionals in various fields but also as socially concious and committed citizens

Our College has earned recognition at the highest levels for its achievements and services. The University Grants Commission granted the College autonomous status from 2006 to 2017. Now the college has returned to the affiliation system of the Kuvempu University. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) has re-accredited the College, with "B" Grade in 2014. While the College feels very proud about such honors, it also sees it as an opportunity to serve the local community with a greater sense of responsibility and dedication.

Studying here, I promise you, will make a great difference to your career and life. Allow us to help you to shape your future and build a strong nation.
